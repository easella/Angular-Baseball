野球ですけど、今はブラウザの中でプレーすることができる。 バッテリーとバッター両方で、ストライクゾーンにクリックすれば、 投球や打球がでる。

簡単に見えるが、野球のファンには作戦が好みだから、このゲームに 出来るだけのそのシミュレーションが含まれている。

守備に入ってから、投球選択で敵バッタを挑戦する。外れすぎだとしたら、バットを振らなくてボール球になる。 逆に真ん中のストレートでホームランになってしまうかもしれない。

攻撃で重要なのはタイミングと目の鋭さ。変化球を見分けるものが勝利をつかむ。

Well, what is this? It's a browser game, the game of baseball. You can play as the battery (pitcher & catcher) when on defense and play as the batter when on offense. Both involve clicking on the strike zone to pitch or bat.

Although simple in this presentation, fans of baseball enjoy a lot of strategic depth in the game and I've tried to model many aspects of it into your web browser.

Starting as the home team on defense, you'll want to mix up your pitches and locate them effectively to keep the batter guessing. Too far off the plate and they won't swing for it, resulting in a ball, but too many pitches down the middle may result in a home run.

Next, on offense, keep a good eye on the ball's breaking trajectory and spin, then swing (click, that is) at the location it crosses the plate. Whether it's a swinging strike, foul, fly, or grounder is up to your timing and coordination. Lay off pitches outside the strike zone or foul them to work the count (pitcher fatigue). Later in the lineup, your team's power hitters can watch a pitch or two go by to better drive the ball.
